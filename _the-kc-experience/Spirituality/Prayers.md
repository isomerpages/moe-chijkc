<p><strong>The prayers commonly used in KC uplift our hearts and minds to God, helping us find strength, peace, and purpose in His presence. They connect us deeply with Jesus, nurture our faith and hope, and remind us of the constant graces and love of God in our daily lives. Through prayer, we grow as a community rooted in faith, guided by the Holy Spirit, and inspired to live out the IJ values of truth, justice, freedom, and love.</strong>
</p>

<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>Prayer of an IJ Girl</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p></p>
<p>O God, like Blessed Nicolas Barre,</p>
<p>may I also believe that you truly love me.</p>
<p>Help me dwell in the sunshine of your great love,</p>
<p>O Lord, so that whatever I may go through in my life, studies, work and
relationships, at home, in school and in society, I may have complete trust
in your love and providence.</p>
<p>Help me to hold firm to your unconditional and excessive love for me and
grant me your peace as I face the challenges on my journey as a citizen
of the world.</p>
<p>Teach me to be generous in using my gifts, talents, expertise and experience
in serving others especially the disadvantaged, the last, the least and
the lost, without counting the cost.</p>
<p>Help me always to respect the dignity of each person I encounter and to
work for the common good. Amen.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>A Prayer of Thanks to God</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p></p>
<p>Our loving Father</p>
<p>thank You for all that You have given us:</p>
<p>our lives, our health and strength;</p>
<p>our homes and our school;</p>
<p>our parents and our friends.</p>
<p>May we never take these for granted,</p>
<p>But rather show our thanks by living our lives as You would wish us to
live them.</p>
<p>Guide each of us in the many choices we will have to make today. Amen.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>Prayer for Wisdom, Courage, Justice</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p></p>
<p>O God,</p>
<p>Give me wisdom always to know what I ought to do, what I ought to say,
where I ought to go. Give me courage to do the right thing especially when
it is difficult.</p>
<p>Give me justice, always to be fair in thought, word and action, never
to be content when anyone is being unjustly treated.</p>
<p>Give me self-control, always to have my impulses, passions and emotions
under control, never to be swept away into doing things for which I would
be sorry, never to do anything which hurt others, grieve those who love
me or bring hardship to myself. Hear this prayer for your loveâ€™s sake.
Amen.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>In Success</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p></p>
<p>O God, I thank you for my success.</p>
<p>Keep me from being proud and arrogant;</p>
<p>keep me always humble.</p>
<p>Help me to remember all those to whom I owe my success, those who taught
and trained me and who encouraged me when I was tired and discouraged.
Help me not to sit back and be complacent but help me to see that there
are still greater heights to climb, and still further goals to reach. Whatever
praise I receive, help me to always be my own greatest critic. Amen.</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>In Disappointments</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p></p>
<p>O God be with me in my time of difficulty. You know how disappointed I
have been this time. The things that I set my heart on were not for me.
The friends I trusted proved untrue.</p>
<p>I honestly tried so hard, and I failed.</p>
<p>Help me O God to accept things as they are. Help me not to waste my time
on vain regrets and unhappy memories. Give me strength to begin again and
to persevere. Help me to always look forward and not back. Help me to forget
the things that are behind and ever to press forward to things which are
ahead. Amen.</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
</div>
</details>
<details class="isomer-details">
<summary><strong>For Friends</strong>
</summary>
<div data-type="detailsContent" class="isomer-details-content">
<p></p>
<p>I thank you for my friends, for those who understand me, for those who
know me at my worst and still love me, for those who have forgiven me when
I let them down.</p>
<p>Help me to be as true to my friends as I would wish them to be to me.</p>
<p>And help me to have no bitterness but only forgiveness for any of my friends
who failed or who turned against me.</p>
<p>Lord, help me to always be kind, loving and caring towards my friends.</p>
<p>And together, grow to be the best we can be to each other and for others.</p>
</div>
</details>
