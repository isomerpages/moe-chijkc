<h4><strong>Use of Mobile Devices (Rules &amp; Regulations)</strong></h4>
<p>1. Students are allowed to bring mobile phones to school. This is to facilitate
parents who wish to contact their child after school. Mobile phones can
only be used before <strong>7:35 am (Mon-Wed &amp; Fri) / 8:35 am (Thurs)</strong> on
the school premises. After school dismissal, mobile phones can only be
used in the canteen and foyer.</p>
<p>2. Students are required to <strong>keep their devices in their personal lockers by 7.35 / 8.35 a.m. (on Thu)</strong>.
Students are <strong><u>not allowed</u></strong> to use their mobile phones
during school hours, including breaks, free periods, afternoon lessons
and CCAs.</p>
<p>3. During curriculum time, parents should call the school office if they
wish to contact their child <strong><u>for urgent reasons</u></strong>.</p>
<p>4. Should mobile phones be heard, seen or used during school hours, the
following consequences will be meted out.</p>

<p>5. Students <strong>must not charge</strong> their mobile phones and PLDs
in school.</p>
<p>6. Students are advised to be <strong><u>responsible for the safekeeping</u></strong> of
their valuables such as PLDs and mobile phones. Students should make use
of the lockers provided to keep their devices; <strong><u>under no circumstances should the devices be left unattended</u></strong>.</p>
<p>7. Smartwatches capable of communication, data storage, photo taking,
photo storage and/or calculation are <strong><u>not allowed</u></strong>.</p>
<p>8. For weighted assessments and examinations (in line with the SEAB guidelines),
any electronic device capable of scanning, storing or displaying visual
and verbal information, including fitness trackers, is <strong><u>not allowed</u></strong>.</p>
