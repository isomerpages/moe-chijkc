<p>With the release of the ‘O’-Level results on 10 January 2025 and the ‘N’-Level
results on 16 December 2024, we would like to offer our heartfelt congratulations
to our Class of 2024!&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>91.4% of our 4N(A) students qualified for 5N(A) and 32.9% attained an
ELMAB3 score of 12 points or less.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>67% of our 4N(T) students secured early admission to ITE and 11% scored
3As or better.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>94.5% of our 4E students are eligible for JC/MI and 99.3% are eligible
for Polytechnic.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>41.2% of our 5N(A) students are eligible for JC/MI and 82.4% are eligible
for Polytechnic.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>13.9% of students successfully placed in the Early Admissions Exercise
to Polytechnic and Direct School Admissions Exercise to Junior College.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Our Principal, Mrs Tan, reminded students that the national exams are
only one of many milestones to follow. For every student, there are always
options available, and the school is here to provide the support needed
to move forward.&nbsp;</p>
<p>We would also like to thank the village behind each student - parents,
guardians, family, teachers and friends who walked the journey with each
of our girls.&nbsp;</p>
<p>All the best for the adventures you will take, and the memories you will
make. Proudly fly the KC flag and continue to develop into Women of Purpose,
simple in virtue and steadfast in duty. Congratulations once again!</p>
