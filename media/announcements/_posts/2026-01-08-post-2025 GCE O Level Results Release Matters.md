<h4>2025 GCE O-Level Results Release Matters<br>The O-Level results will be released on Wednesday, 14 January 2026.</h4>
<p><strong>Collection of GCE O-Level Results</strong>
</p>
<p>Please note the following arrangements:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are to be in their school uniform.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students and accompanying parent(s)/ guardian(s) are to be seated in the
hall by <strong>1.35 pm</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who are unable to collect their results in person may arrange
for <strong>their parent/guardian</strong> to collect the results on their
behalf, <strong>or appoint a proxy (aged 21 and above)</strong> to collect
a physical copy of their results from the school. <strong>Please submit the online form</strong> if
you are appointing a proxy; or if your parents/ guardians will be collecting
the results on your behalf <a href="https://forms.gle/sNfq9uaBXSCLAjtx7" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://forms.gle/sNfq9uaBXSCLAjtx7</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>For appointment of proxy</strong>:
<br><strong>a) Please fill in the details of the proxy in the attached proxy form</strong>
<br>b) The person whom you authorize to collect your result slip and other
documents on your behalf, <strong>must bring along the proxy form duly completed and signed</strong> as
well as <strong>his/her NRIC/ FIN/ card</strong> for verification purposes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please note that <strong>parent/ guardian/ authorized personnel can only collect th results from 3.00 pm on the day of results release in the General Office</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents/ Guardians are advised to park at the nearby HDB carparks.</p>
</li>
</ol>
