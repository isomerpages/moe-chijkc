<h3><strong><u>2024 GCE O-Level Results Release Matters</u></strong></h3>
<h4>The O-Level results will be released on Friday, 10 January 2025<br><br><strong>Collection of GCE O-Level Results</strong><br>Please note the following arrangements:</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students and accompanying parent(s)/ guardian(s) are to be seated in the
school hall by 2.00pm.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to be properly attired in school uniform.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who are unable to collect their results in person can appoint
a proxy to collect a physical copy of their results from the school.&nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Please fill in the details of the proxy in the attached <strong><a href="/files/Announcements/Authorisation_Form_CHIJ_KC.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">authorisation form</a></strong>  <strong>and</strong> submit
the <strong><a href="https://go.gov.sg/4e5nproxy2024" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">online form</a></strong> for
our forward planning.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The person, whom you authorize to collect your result slip and other documents
on your behalf, <strong>must bring along the authorization form duly completed and signed </strong>as
well as <strong>his/her identity card </strong>for verification purposes.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please note that the authorized personnel can only collect the result
from 3.30 pm on the day of results release in the Music Room.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents/ Guardians are advised to park at the nearby HDB carparks.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h4><strong>Resources for Parents</strong></h4>
<p>Receiving exam results and making choices for post-secondary education
can be a stressful time for students. The social and emotional support
you provide is important in helping our teens manage their emotions and
decide their next steps.&nbsp;
<br>
<br>For resources on providing social and emotional support, and supporting
your teen’s transition to post-secondary education, you may refer to the
following resources:&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="https://go.gov.sg/selresforparents" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>https://go.gov.sg/selresforparents</u></a>&nbsp;
