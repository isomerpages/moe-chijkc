<p><strong><u>2025 GCE N-Level Results Release Matters</u></strong>
<br>The N-Level results will be released on <strong>Thursday, 18 December 2025, at 2.00pm</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Collection of GCE N-Level Results</strong>
<br>Please note the following arrangements:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students are to be in their school uniform.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students and accompanying parent(s)/ guardian(s) are to be seated in the
library by 1.30 pm.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who are unable to collect their results in person may arrange
for<strong> their parent/guardian </strong>to collect the results on their
behalf, <strong>or appoint a proxy (aged 21 and above)</strong> to collect
a physical copy of their results from the school. <strong>Please submit the online form</strong> if
you are appointing a proxy; or if your parents/ guardians will be collecting
the results on your behalf <a href="https://go.gov.sg/kcnlevelresults2025" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>https://go.gov.sg/kcnlevelresults2025</u></a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>For appointment of proxy:</strong>
<br><strong>a) Please fill in the details of the proxy in the attached proxy form</strong>
<br><strong>b)&nbsp;</strong>The person whom you authorize to collect your
result slip and other documents on your behalf, <strong>must bring along the proxy form duly completed and signed </strong>as
well as <strong>his/her NRIC/ FIN/ card </strong>for verification purposes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Please note that <strong>parent/ guardian/ authorized personnel can only collect the results from 3.00 pm on the day of results release in the Music Room.</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Parents/ Guardians are advised to park at the nearby HDB carparks.
