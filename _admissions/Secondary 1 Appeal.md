<p><strong>Students posted to other schools but wish to transfer to CHIJ Katong Convent</strong>
<br>
<br>Students appealing for admission to KC are required to meet the cut-off
points for the relevant Posting Group for their transfer appeals to be
considered, subject to availability of vacancy.
<br>
<br><strong>How do I apply?</strong>
<br>Application will be open in December (Dates to be confirmed in December)
and to be done strictly via this link -&nbsp;<a href="https://form.gov.sg/admin/form/69f8474826c23ea1f71626db" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Sec 1 Appeal</a>.</p>
