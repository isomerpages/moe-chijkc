<h3>A Woman of Purpose</h3>
<p>As part of the school’s character development focus, Katong Convent is
committed in developing the all-round KC girl.</p>
<p>Each student is being developed in relation to the competencies of the
KC Graduate Profile comprising 4 main domains:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Compassionate Servant Leader</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Innovative Problem Solver</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Effective Communicator</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Discerning Citizen of the World</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The attributes of a KC Graduate form part of the journey in the development
of our students. They permeate key programmes and events and students regularly
pause, reflect on and receive feedback from their teachers.</p>
