<h4>Financial Assistance Scheme (FAS)</h4>
<p>Singapore Citizens in financial need can get assistance from MOE on school
fees and other expenses. Parents who wish to know more about MOE FAS can
access the MOE website at&nbsp;<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/news/press-releases/20251016-MOE-Financial-Assistance-Schemes-to-Benefit-an-Additional-31000-Students" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Information on Income Eligibility Criteria (revised October 2025)</a>.</p>
<p>Parents can apply for MOE FAS using the e-FAS form, which is available
on Parents Gateway (under Services tab â€“ Financial Assistance) using Singpass
login or via the following link&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/moe-efas" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/moe-efas.</a>&nbsp;</p>

<p>For more information, please click&nbsp;<a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/financial-matters/financial-assistance" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
<p>If you have children in different government or government-aided schools,
you just need to submit 1 application form to any of their schools. The
school that receives the application form will inform you of the outcome.</p>
<p>If you have any queries, please contact the school at Tel: 6448 6433 or
you can email us at&nbsp;<a href="mailto:chijktcs@moe.edu.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">chijktcs@moe.edu.sg</a>.</p>
